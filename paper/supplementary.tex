\section{Complex-Valued Channel Estimation}\label{sec:supp_complex}

To validate whether the error concentration mechanism transfers to the complex domain, we implement a complex-valued LISTA variant with magnitude-based soft-thresholding:
\begin{equation}
\mathcal{S}_\theta(\mathbf{z}) = \mathbf{z} \cdot \max\left(1 - \frac{\theta}{|\mathbf{z}|}, 0\right)
\end{equation}
where $\mathbf{z} \in \mathbb{C}^N$ and the operator preserves phase while thresholding magnitude. The complex LISTA uses separate real and imaginary weight matrices ($\mathbf{W}^{(k)}_{\text{re}}, \mathbf{W}^{(k)}_{\text{im}} \in \mathbb{R}^{N \times N}$). Training uses QPSK pilots and complex-valued channels with the same mixed-SNR protocol.
